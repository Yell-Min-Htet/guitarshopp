Details


Cordoba classical guitars are known for their lightweight build and resonant tone, which is attributed to the use of solid woods such as cedar, spruce, and mahogany for the tops, backs, and sides. They also use traditional fan bracing, a construction method that enhances the guitar's resonance and tonal quality. Many Cordoba models feature a slim neck and slightly thinner body, making them easier to play, especially for guitarists transitioning from steel-string guitars.
